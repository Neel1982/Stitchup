About StitchUp

StitchUp empowers home-based tailors, focusing on women entrepreneurs, by providing a digital platform for on-demand services. We address market disorganization, privacy, and sustainability through verified profiles, gender preferences, and upcycling promotions.

Future Scope

  • AI Fashion Assistant: Personalized style recommendations using machine learning.
  • Global Marketplace: Expand to international tailors and customers.
  • AR Virtual Try-On: Enhanced measurements and previews.
  • Social Impact: Franchise hubs for training and community building.

Business Model

  • Commission on bookings (10-15%).
  • Premium subscriptions for tailors (featured profiles).
  • Affiliate sales for fabrics and tools via partnered e-commerce.
